The One Reason Why Gala

Come be a part of the story. The mission of Ten Thousand Villages is to create opportunities for artisans in developing countries to earn income by bringing their products and stories to our markets through long-term, fair-trading relationships.Please join us at our annual One Reason Why fundraising gala event. It’s held every April, and we take time to celebrate fair trade with a globally inspired evening of art and entertainment.

Global Austin

What started in 1960 as a simple program to welcome international students to the University of Texas campus, is now part of the U.S. Department of State's International Visitor Leadership Program (IVLP). We've connected thousands of international visitors with those in Austin who forge our economy, architecture, environment. You’re invited to join the GlobalAustin community for a night of fun, and shopping as we host our annual Benefit night this year at Ten Thousand Villages.

MAC Cares

Cares is a group of committed parents and community members who provide support through McCallum’s Licensed Mental Health Professional (LMHP) and counselors to the students they serve. Join us for MACare’s annual fundraiser. Performances by MAC Saxes, Ukulele Road Trip, and McCallum vocalists. Admission is free. 11.4.21

Tree Folk Benefit

TreeFolks has engaged thousands of volunteers to plant more than 2.8 million trees in Central Texas. Planting trees is a powerful means of connecting people with their community . Trees clean the air and water, provide habitat for wildlife, produce fresh fruit for neighbors, and shelter us from the hot Texas sun. Join us for Treefolks annual fundraiser. This year Ten Thousand Villages Austin has generously offered to host the event. Admission is free. There will be lots of fabulous shopping, entertainment and – free wine!
